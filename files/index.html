Vision & Mission

Completing the Great Commission

IM Mission exists to fulfill the mission of completing evangelism to all nations. We raise every person as a missionary, equipped to carry out God's calling in their place, region, domain, and to the ends of the earth.

Through our Triple School system — Gospel School, Disciple School, and Calling School — we develop whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionaries who transform the world from the inside out.
