Ministry 01 — Education

Raising the next generation
of missionaries

The next generation is the foundation of the church. IM Mission raises up Re:NewGen — a new generation that leads a new breed and starts a new wave. We equip every person as a whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionary through education, mentoring, and community.

"Raising whole-person Christians through the Triple School System"

Gospel School · Disciple School · Calling School

01. Gospel School

Raising whole-person Christians

Establishing a Christian worldview through three perspectives: Redemptive History (God's history of salvation), Kingdom of God (God's purpose of salvation), and Covenant (God's method of salvation).

02. Disciple School

Raising whole-life Christians

Establishing a Christian value system through godly thinking, godly action, and godly living — grounded in the truth of Scripture in every area of life.

03. Calling School

Raising frontline Christians

Establishing Christian influence through vision and calling, evangelism and mission, and church and end times — equipping missionaries to impact the world.

Re:NewGen Community

Leading the 4th Industrial Revolution

Harmonizing online systems and community systems to form a new generation that navigates digital and physical worlds with a kingdom perspective.

Raising Global Leaders

Connected to the Moving School and worldwide communities through Sedasal, forming global leaders who carry the gospel across nations.

Protecting Christian Education

Establishing worldview, values, and calling through the Triple School — raising up a generation with strong biblical foundations.

Ministry 03 — Local Church

Connecting the church
and the mission

The local church is a modality — focused on congregation and nurture. The mission society is a sodality — focused on mission and task. IM Mission continuously supports and collaborates with Korean local churches so that the task of missionary training becomes embedded in the local church, enabling them to actively participate in God's kingdom mission strategy.

"Building Moso-Dality — churches that plant missionaries in every generation"

Sodality + Modality working together for the kingdom

01. Next-Generation Mission Partnership

A structure that allows local churches to experience next-generation mission through the mission society, then carry it out directly in their own church context.

Online System

Go-B/Com-B · Seminar/Conference · Parent School · Na-Geurisdoin · CBUP

A comprehensive online system connecting churches to IM Mission's training resources, allowing any church to access next-gen mission tools anytime.

Offline System

Church Independence Camp · Renew-Gen Community · Jesus Copy Camp · Handal Camp

Offline gatherings and camps that bring churches into direct, embodied community — experiencing transformation together and sending missionaries from within.

Ministry 04 — Mission

Every Christian,
a missionary

By expanding the meaning of "missionary" and "mission," IM Mission raises all Christians as missionaries and designates every place of life as a mission field. The gospel, education, nurture, and mission are reproduced in every local area, domain, and nation.

"Wherever you stand is your mission field"

Local · Urban · Domain · Nations — every Christian is a missionary

01

Local Mission

My place becomes my mission field

Home · Workplace · School · Church · Community — every place where a Christian stands is a mission field. IM Mission equips believers to live as missionaries in the ordinary places of everyday life.

02

Urban Evangelization

Transforming cities with the gospel

In the era of the 4th Industrial Revolution and the new normal, the boundaries between virtual and real, online and offline, and different generations are blurring. IntuGen — bridging generations and realities — breaks through cities with the gospel.

03

Domain Mission

God's reign in every sphere of society

Politics · Economy · Education · Arts & Sports · Media · Medicine & Science & Technology — IM Mission raises missionaries who bring God's kingdom principles into every domain of society, transforming culture from within.

04

Nations Mission

The gospel crossing every border

IM Mission has established its overseas mission headquarters in the Philippines as a model base for how IM's vision and ministry structure can be implemented in global missions. A US branch leads missions among Korean students, next-generation ministry in America, and South American mission work. Networks of missionaries are formed worldwide through Moving School, short-term missions, study abroad, and online ministry — enabling continuous nations missions.
