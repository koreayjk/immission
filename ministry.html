Ministry 01

Education — Raising the next generation

The next generation is the foundation of the church. IM Mission raises up Re:NewGen through the Triple School system — forming whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionaries through spiritual formation, discipleship, and calling.

"IM Mission's Triple School trains whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionaries — Gospel School, Disciple School, and Calling School."

복음스쿨 · 제자스쿨 · 소명스쿨

01 — Gospel School

Raising whole-person Christians

Establishing a Christian worldview through three lenses: Redemptive History (God's plan of salvation), Kingdom of God (God's purpose), and Covenant (God's method) — rooting every believer in the full story of Scripture.

02 — Disciple School

Raising whole-life Christians

Establishing Christian values through godly thinking, godly action, and godly living — so every area of life is shaped by biblical truth, forming people of deep integrity and character in every season of life.

03 — Calling School

Raising frontline Christians

Establishing Christian influence through vision and calling, evangelism and mission, and church and end times — sending missionaries who transform the world from the inside out.

Re:NewGen Community

IM Mission declares Re:NewGen — a new generation that raises up a new breed and starts a new wave. Through three dimensions of community life, we equip the next generation to lead in every sphere of society.

Leading the 4th Industrial Revolution

Harmonizing online and community systems — forming a generation that navigates digital and physical worlds with a kingdom perspective, using technology for God's glory.

Raising Global Leaders — Sedasal Connection

Connected to the Moving School and global communities worldwide, forming leaders who carry the gospel across nations. The next generation goes to every corner of the earth.

Protecting Christian Education

Through the Triple School, establishing biblical worldview, values, and calling — raising up a generation with foundations that cannot be shaken, equipped to defend and share the truth.

IM Mission

Five Pillars — What we believe and live by

Every morning, the IM Mission community declares these five pillars together. These are not merely statements — they are the living DNA of everything we do. IM 5대 강령 — 신조, 목적, 정신, 사명, 생활원리.

01

Creed — 신조

개혁주의 5대 표어: 솔라 스크립투라, 솔라 그라티아, 솔루스 크리스토스, 솔라 피데, 솔리 데오 글로리아

IM follows the Reformed faith, declaring the five Solas as our creed: Sola Scriptura (Scripture alone) · Sola Gratia (Grace alone) · Solus Christus (Christ alone) · Sola Fide (Faith alone) · Soli Deo Gloria (To God alone be glory).

02

Purpose — 목적

선교완성을 목표로, 각 사람을 선교사로 세워 선교의 사명을 완수하게 함

To work toward the completion of the Great Commission — equipping every person as a missionary to fulfill the mission of God. We exist to prepare for the Lord's final day by raising up frontline missionaries for every nation.

03

Spirit — 정신

복음, 제자, 소명을 정신으로

Gospel · Discipleship · Calling — the core spirit and identity of IM Mission. Through the Triple School, we form people who are rooted in the gospel, shaped as disciples, and launched in their unique God-given calling.

04

Mission — 사명

진리 수호, 진리 전수 · 전인격·전인생·전방향 선교사 양성 · 열방의 복음화

Three-fold mission: to guard and transmit truth; to raise whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionaries; and to evangelize the nations. We are guardians of truth, trainers of missionaries, and senders to the ends of the earth.

05

Life Principles — 생활원리

영성을 겸비하자 · 인격을 겸비하자 · 실력을 겸비하자 · 복음을 전파하자

Grow in spirituality · Grow in character · Grow in competence · Spread the gospel. These four daily principles guide every member of IM Mission — in community, in study, and on the mission field.

Why IM Mission

12 advantages of IM Mission ministry

A mission society that any church can easily access and apply. Here are 12 distinctive advantages that make IM Mission the right partner for your church's next-generation mission. (IM선교회 사역의 12가지 장점)

Advantage 01

Accessibility

IM Mission simplifies, models, and synchronizes its systems so that any local church can adopt and activate next-gen mission on their own — lowering every barrier to entry.

Advantage 02

Moso-Dality

A global generation together with a modality local church creates sodality-level task-focus and mission dedication — the best of both worlds for kingdom advance.

Advantage 03

Real Transformation

Through education and camp ministry, the worldview and values of the next generation are genuinely changed — producing lasting fruit in vision, purpose, and attitude.

Advantage 04

Community

The heart of change is community. Through community, we discover God, ourselves, and others — training in cooperation that transforms individuals and groups alike.

Advantage 05

Peer Community (또래)

Communities have peer-peers (또또), peer-leaders (상또), and Jesus-influencers (미또) — a rich peer ecosystem where the next generation disciples each other.

Advantage 06

Beyond Local Walls

Not limited to individual churches — operating nationwide and worldwide through community networks, shared mission, support ministry, and collaborative outreach.

Advantage 07

Online — 7 Channels

Online education and communication to save and send the next generation nationally and globally — 7 online channels including CBUP, Parent School, Na-Geurisdoin, and Church Support Center.

Advantage 08

Nations Mission

Community networks worldwide ensure Korean mission does not stop at home — enabling continuous, reproducing nations mission that outlasts any single missionary or church.

Advantage 09

English — Nations Weapon

Running English education systems as a foundational tool, always ready to advance to nations mission — equipping the next generation to communicate the gospel across every language barrier.

Advantage 10

Reformed Theological Base

Through the Reformed Triple School, raising spiritually strong leaders who carry robust biblical theology, sound doctrine, and deep knowledge of God's redemptive story.

Advantage 11

Movement, Not Institution

The vision to save the next generation, revive local churches, and spark mission revival is carried out not as a top-down institution but as a self-initiated, Spirit-driven movement.

Advantage 12

Young Ministers, Field Ministry

Ministers whose hearts beat fastest for God — working with their hands and running with their feet in frontline, field-centered ministry that does not wait for permission to move.
