Vision & Mission

Completing the Great Commission

IM Mission exists to fulfill the mission of completing evangelism to all nations. We raise every person as a missionary, equipped to carry out God's calling in their place, region, domain, and to the ends of the earth.

Through our Triple School system — Gospel School, Disciple School, and Calling School — we develop whole-person, whole-life, frontline missionaries who transform the world from the inside out.

God's History

IM Mission
since 2006

From a small youth community in Daejeon, God has grown IM Mission into an international movement spanning Korea, Philippines, USA, India, and beyond.

2006Founded in Daejeon · Michael English School established · Youth community ministry begins
2007Renamed IM Mission · English Vision Camp launched · IEM International School founded
2009First India short-term mission · Daejeon & Cheonan branch English schools opened
2013Philippines ANTS Seminary opens · IM Philippines branch established · MTS Youth School begins
2016Handal (한다살) national movement launched · CBUP educator training program starts
2018IM Overseas Mission HQ established in Philippines · CBUP 1st cohort graduates
2019Missions to USA, Mexico, China, Taiwan, Singapore · World Conference launched online
2020–Renew-Gen Community system · Han·Da·Se strategy · Brazil, Russia, Mongolia missions
